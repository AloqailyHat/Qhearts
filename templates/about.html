About us

Welcome to Qassim Animal Rescue!

Our Story

Founded in 2016, Qassim Animal Rescue is a passionate group of animal lovers dedicated to making a difference in the lives of our furry friends. Our journey began with a simple yet powerful idea: to create a safe haven for animals in need right here in the heart of Qassim.

Our Mission

At Qassim Animal Rescue, our mission is clear: to rescue, rehabilitate, and re-home animals. We believe that every life matters, whether it's a playful kitten, a loyal dog, or a gentle rabbit. Our commitment extends beyond mere rescue; we strive to provide medical care, emotional support, and a second chance at life for each precious soul that crosses our path.

What Drives Us

Our founder, Maram, is the heartbeat of our organization. Driven by an unwavering love for animals, they embarked on this journey with a vision to create a better world for our four-legged companions. Their tireless efforts have inspired our team and community to join hands in this noble cause.
